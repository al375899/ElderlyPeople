Â¿What is ElderlyPeople?

Program aimed at improving the living conditions of the elderly who remain in their homes, providing them with basic services: daily food, washing bed linen and towels, as well as cleaning the home, which contributes decisively to improving their health, well-being and quality of life.
The aim is to make it easier for the elderly to stay in their home and / or family and social environment for as long as possible.
The social program "Major at home" includes a set of services and supplies related to each other that are provided in a coordinated and continuous manner at the user's home In the calendar month. These services are: health, food at home, cleanning, others such as accompaniment, company, ...
